Features

🏗️

Procedural Architecture

Each project category gets a unique silhouette — helix towers, data shards, ziggurats, and more.

🔮

Neural Core

Central wireframe sphere pulses with city activity. RGB chromatic split. The heartbeat of your workspace.

🌀

Data Arteries

Animated neon tubes flow from the core to buildings when files change. See your city breathe.

🖱️

Click to Navigate

Click any building to open its note. Double-click to reposition the camera. Your vault at a glance.

🤖

Launch AI Agents

Right-click buildings or the Neural Core to spawn Claude Code terminals with full project context.

⚡

Real-Time Activity

Watch your AI work in real-time — streaming arteries, pulsing core, and a live activity overlay.
